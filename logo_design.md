>
> The top banner text must read: **“West Sacramento Experimental Trophic Laboratory”**

>
> In the center of the emblem is a realistic depiction of the **Tower Bridge in Sacramento, California**, rendered accurately with correct angles and architectural detail. Beneath the bridge is a vibrant underwater ecosystem scene, as if the river opens into an ocean environment.
>
> Include a **large realistic whale** swimming in the water along with **small colorful tropical fish** and **coral**, all rendered with professional, naturalistic shading consistent with scientific illustration. The fish and coral should blend seamlessly with the underwater environment and match the realism of the whale.
>
> The style should be **clean, institutional, and highly professional**, not cartoonish or playful. Colors should be **deep ocean blues, teal water tones, and subtle coral colors**, with balanced lighting and smooth gradients to create depth and realism.

> The outer ring should resemble a **government research seal**, with elegant typography, subtle metallic accents, and crisp vector-style lines. The design should look like an official emblem used by a research laboratory or environmental agency.
>
> The composition should feel authoritative, scientific, and slightly surreal because of the inland ocean imagery.
