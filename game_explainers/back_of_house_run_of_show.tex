\begin{labbox}{Host Reminders}
\begin{itemize}
\item Teams of 4 receive \textbf{+\$150}; log it early..
\item Data Scientist may call \textbf{Model Overfit} before a mini-game; that match pays double.
\item Dolphin Trainer requires the team member with the \textbf{Dolphin} role to be the dolphin.
\item Oceanographers may search for hidden cash during play.
\item Whale prestige is worth \textbf{+\$200 per whale} at final scoring.
\item Squid theft happens \textbf{after} proposal awards and before winner announcement.
\end{itemize}
\end{labbox}

\begin{labbox}{Scoring Closeout}
\begin{enumerate}
\item Stop station play hard at 9:30 and collect any unresolved match results.
\item Verify mini-game wins include an opposing signature before counting them.
\item Add hidden discoveries, team-size bonus, completion bonus, whale prestige, and proposal awards.
\item Reveal squid phase; each squid steals \textbf{\$200} from one rival lab.
\item Recalculate totals after all squid transfers are applied.
\item Double-check top two labs before announcing winners.
\end{enumerate}
\end{labbox}
