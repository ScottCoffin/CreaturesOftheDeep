
# 🐬 Dolphin Trainer Task Cards

---

### 1. The Social Butterfly

Dolphin must:

* high-five **3 different people**
---

### 2. Fashion Evolution

Dolphin must:

* put on **hat + sunglasses**

---

### 3. Baton Ceremony

Dolphin must:

* pick up baton
* hand it to a stranger
* receive it back

---

### 4. Percussionist

Dolphin must:

* play **drum or maraca**
* for at least 5 beats

---

### 5. Dramatic Entrance

Dolphin must:

* leave the area
* re-enter dramatically (spin, strut, etc.)

---

### 6. The Recruiter

Dolphin must:

* bring **one new person** back to the team

---

### 7. Hydration Protocol

Dolphin must:

* obtain a drink
* take one sip

---

### 8. Interpretive Dance

Dolphin must:

* perform a **5-second dance**
* using the hand fan

---

### 9. The Messenger

Dolphin must:

* deliver a message (“hello” is fine)
* to someone across the room

---

### 10. Sunglass Transfer

Dolphin must:

* put on sunglasses
* then give them to someone else

---

### 11. The Parade Leader

Dolphin must:

* lead a line of **2 people**
* for at least 3 steps

---

### 12. Object Collector

Dolphin must:

* gather **2 different objects**
* bring them back

---


### 13. Freeze Frame

Dolphin must:

* pose like a statue for 5 seconds

---

### 14. Sound Maker

Dolphin must:

* make any **non-verbal noise**
* 3 times

---

### 15. The Exchange

Dolphin must:

* trade one item with someone
* and bring back the new item

---

### 16. Hat Relay

Dolphin must:

* wear hat
* pass it to 2 different people

---

### 17. The Spiral

Dolphin must:

* spin in a circle
* then sit or crouch

---

### 18. The Escort

Dolphin must:

* guide someone by gesture
* back to the team

---

### 19. The Performer

Dolphin must:

* use drum or maraca
* and get at least 1 person to join

---

### 20. The Scientist

Dolphin must:

* point at 3 different people
* as if “observing specimens”

---

### 21. The Mirror

Dolphin must:

* copy another person’s movement
* for 5 seconds

---

### 22. Fan Operator

Dolphin must:

* use hand fan on someone
* for 3 seconds

---

### 23. The Trade Route

Dolphin must:

* take an object from Team A
* give it to Team B

---



### 24. The Return Mission

Dolphin must:

* leave the team
* touch a wall or object
* return

---


### 25. The Mystery Object

Dolphin must:

* pick up a random object
* present it to the team

---

### 26. The Mating Display

Dolphin must:

* kiss someone (cheek is fine)
